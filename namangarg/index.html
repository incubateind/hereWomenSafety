Frequently Asked Questions

What is Online Challenge?
Online challenge is an event where people come together to solve problems on an online platform. It means you can solve problems given to you by implementing ideas into play, codes in action by creative and concentrated way.
What is Hackathon?
Hackathon is an event where people come together to solve problems. It means you bring ideas into play, in a collaborative, creative and concentrated way. Hackathon is great because you push yourself further - and often meet friends on the way.
Who can attend?
The Online Challenge is open for everyone. However, in case of hackathon, we can’t host students under 18 overnight, due to legal issues.
What if I don't have a team or idea?
We got you. We’ll support you all with team formation sessions and brainstorming for ideas, so that you can form a team of max 4 members. Its encouraging to pair up with people who posses different skills. According to our experience, Diverse Background generates CREATIVE projects!
What does it cost?
Our event is free for all admitted hackers. We provide meals, coffee, swag, WiFi and electricity, etc. Unfortunately, we don't have the resources to provide travel reimbursements this year.
Do I need to know how to code?
Skills like Thinking, Coding, Designing and Problem Solving are helpful in a Hackathon but this is also a perfect place/time to begin learning.
Who will shortlist the teams?
The process of shortlisting will be carried out by an esteemed panel of industry professionals who'll also be mentoring the teams in this competition.
Other Questions.
You can message or email us! Reach out to us through the contact form at the bottom of the page.

IncubateIND

By providing a space for the next generation of explorers to discover, create, and work together to achieve collective goals and pursue shared ambitions, IncubateIND provides fertile ground for networking and teamwork. We work on solving world’s greatest problems through technology driven community of entrepreneurs, student and government. IncubateIND will be an educational experience for participants of all levels. We will have various workshops like Educational, Drone, health, bots, cyber security etc. from which participants can learn entirely new skills or platforms, and many mentors who will be able to share their knowledge acquired with years of experience.
